Trading Guides

Step-by-step instructions for every scenario

BUYER · MAKER

I want to buy Bitcoin

You create the order, set the terms, and wait for a seller to take it.

Open Tor→ Generate Robot→ Create Order→ Lock Bond→ Wait for Taker→ Send Fiat→ Receive Sats ⚡
1

Open RoboSats via Tor

Open Tor Browser and navigate to the Ammanaya onion address:

ammannjgzybw4qm2odmci7xgolh5grzijidacjxose5tthm375dcopad.onion

💡 Bookmark the address for quick access. Never use clearnet.
2

Generate Your Robot

A random robot identity is automatically generated for you. You'll see a unique robot avatar and name.

⚠️ Save your robot token immediately! It's the ONLY way to recover your identity. Write it down or copy it to a safe place. If you lose it, your robot is gone forever.
3

Create a BUY Order

Click "Create" and configure your order:

• Type: Buy
• Amount: How much fiat you want to spend (e.g. 50 EUR)
• Currency: Your fiat currency (EUR, USD, etc.)
• Payment method: How you'll send fiat (Bizum, Revolut, bank transfer, etc.)
• Premium: % above/below market price you're willing to pay. 0% = market price. Positive = you pay more (fills faster). Negative = you pay less (takes longer).

💡 A small positive premium (1-3%) makes your order more attractive to sellers and fills faster.
4

Lock Your Fidelity Bond

RoboSats generates a Lightning hold invoice for your fidelity bond (default: 3% of the trade amount). Pay it with your Lightning wallet.

The bond is not spent — it's locked temporarily and returned to you after the trade completes successfully. It only gets forfeited if you cheat or abandon the trade.

💡 Use a wallet that supports hold invoices: Phoenix, Zeus, Blixt, Electrum, Alby. Check wallet compatibility.
5

Wait for a Seller

Your order is now live in the order book. A seller will see it and can take it. This may take minutes or hours depending on your premium and the market.

You can enable notifications via Telegram (@ammanaya_bot) or use in-app notifications so you don't have to keep the page open.

💡 You can cancel the order at any time before someone takes it — your bond is returned.
6

Seller Takes Your Order

When a seller takes your order, they lock their bond AND the trade escrow (the sats you're buying). Both sides are now committed.

You'll see a trade chat where you can communicate with the seller.

7

Submit Your Payout Invoice

Provide a Lightning invoice from your wallet for the amount of sats you're buying. This is where you'll receive the Bitcoin once the trade completes.

⚠️ Make sure the invoice hasn't expired and has enough capacity in your wallet to receive.
8

Send Fiat Payment

Send the agreed fiat amount to the seller via the payment method you specified (Bizum, Revolut, bank transfer, etc.). Follow any instructions the seller provides in the chat.

Once sent, click "Confirm fiat sent" in the trade interface.

⚠️ Only send fiat to the details provided in the RoboSats chat. Never communicate outside the platform.
9

Receive Your Sats ⚡

The seller confirms they received your fiat payment. The escrowed sats are instantly released to your Lightning invoice. Your bond is returned.

🎉 Done! You just bought Bitcoin privately with no KYC.

BUYER · TAKER

I want to take an existing Sell order

Someone is already selling — you pick their order and buy their sats.

Open Tor→ Generate Robot→ Browse Order Book→ Take Order→ Lock Bond→ Submit Invoice→ Send Fiat→ Receive Sats ⚡
1

Open RoboSats & Generate Robot

Open Tor Browser, go to the Ammanaya onion address, and generate your robot. Save your token!

2

Browse the Order Book

Go to the order book and look for SELL orders. Filter by your currency and preferred payment method.

Check the premium — this is how much above/below market price the seller is asking. Lower premium = better deal for you.

💡 You can also filter by amount range to find orders that match how much you want to spend.
3

Take the Order

Found a good offer? Click on the order and hit "Take Order". You'll need to specify the exact fiat amount if the order has a range.

4

Lock Your Fidelity Bond

Pay the bond hold invoice (3% of trade amount) with your Lightning wallet. The seller has already locked their bond AND the escrow when they created the order.

5

Submit Your Payout Invoice

Provide a Lightning invoice from your wallet where you want to receive the sats.

6

Send Fiat & Confirm

Send fiat to the seller using the agreed payment method. Click "Confirm fiat sent" when done.

7

Receive Your Sats ⚡

Seller confirms receipt of fiat → sats released to your invoice → bonds returned. 🎉 Done!

SELLER · MAKER

I want to sell Bitcoin

You create the order, set the terms, and wait for a buyer to take it.

Open Tor→ Generate Robot→ Create Order→ Lock Bond + Escrow→ Wait for Buyer→ Receive Fiat→ Confirm 💶
1

Open RoboSats & Generate Robot

Open Tor Browser, go to the Ammanaya onion address, and generate your robot. Save your token!

2

Create a SELL Order

Click "Create" and configure:

• Type: Sell
• Amount: How much fiat you want to receive (e.g. 100 EUR)
• Currency: The fiat currency you want
• Payment method: How you want to receive fiat (Bizum, Revolut, etc.)
• Premium: % above market price. Higher premium = you get more fiat per sat, but order may take longer to fill.

3

Lock Bond + Trade Escrow

As a seller, you pay two hold invoices:

• Fidelity bond (3%) — returned after successful trade
• Trade escrow — the actual sats you're selling, held until buyer sends fiat

⚠️ Make sure your Lightning wallet has enough balance for both the bond and the escrow combined.
4

Wait for a Buyer

Your order is live. A buyer will see it and can take it. Enable notifications to know when someone does.

5

Buyer Takes Your Order

A buyer locks their bond and the trade begins. They submit their payout invoice and then send you fiat.

6

Receive Fiat & Confirm

Check your payment method (bank account, Bizum, Revolut, etc.) for the incoming fiat payment.

Once you've verified the payment is in your account, click "Confirm fiat received".

⚠️ NEVER confirm fiat received before it's actually in your account. Check for pending vs. completed status. Scammers may show fake screenshots.
7

Trade Complete 🎉

Once you confirm, the escrowed sats are released to the buyer. Your bond is returned. You've sold Bitcoin privately.

SELLER · TAKER

I want to take an existing Buy order

Someone wants to buy sats — you sell to them by taking their order.

Open Tor→ Generate Robot→ Browse Order Book→ Take Order→ Lock Bond + Escrow→ Receive Fiat→ Confirm 💶
1

Open RoboSats & Generate Robot

Open Tor Browser, go to the Ammanaya onion address, and generate your robot. Save your token!

2

Browse the Order Book

Look for BUY orders — these are people who want to buy sats from you. Filter by currency and payment method.

💡 BUY orders with a high positive premium mean the buyer is willing to pay more — good deal for you as seller!
3

Take the Order

Click on the order and hit "Take Order".

4

Lock Bond + Trade Escrow

As taker-seller, you pay the fidelity bond (3%) and the trade escrow (the sats the buyer is purchasing). Two hold invoices.

5

Buyer Sends Fiat

The buyer sends fiat via the payment method specified in the order. Wait for the payment to arrive in your account.

6

Confirm Fiat Received

Verify the fiat is actually in your account (not just pending). Then click "Confirm fiat received".

⚠️ Always verify the payment is fully settled before confirming. Once confirmed, sats are released and cannot be reversed.
7

Trade Complete 🎉

Sats released to buyer, bonds returned to both parties. You've sold Bitcoin privately with no KYC.
