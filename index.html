For convenience, enable Telegram notifications via @ammanaya_bot from your robot profile. For maximum privacy, use only the native in-app notifications — enable them for Ammanaya (or any coordinator you use) in your robot settings.

Pay a small bond (3%) via Lightning. Once matched, send fiat to the seller via the agreed method. When confirmed, sats are released to your wallet instantly.

How to Sell Bitcoin

Convert your sats to fiat privately.

1

Open Ammanaya in Tor

Same URL: ammannjgzybw4qm2odmci7xgolh5grzijidacjxose5tthm375dcopad.onion

2

Create a SELL Order

Set the amount, currency, payment method you want to receive fiat through, and your premium.

3

Lock Bond + Escrow

Pay the bond (3%) plus the trade escrow (the sats you're selling) via Lightning hold invoice.

4

Receive Fiat

The buyer sends fiat via the agreed payment method. Confirm receipt in the trade chat.

5

Sats Released to Buyer

Once you confirm fiat received, the escrowed sats are released to the buyer. Your bond is returned. Done!
